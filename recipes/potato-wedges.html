Potato Wedges

By Tiffany from Creme De La Crumb

Description

These are easy, crispy oven-baked potato wedges with a savory-seasoned exterior and a soft fluffy interior, served with a simple and tasty sauce. Makes around 6 servings.

Ingredients

For the potatoes:

  • 2 lb red potatoes (about 6-8 red potatoes)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tps salt
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika (may sub in regular paprika)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • Pinch of black pepper

For the dipping sauce:

  • 1/3 cup mayo
  • 1/3 cup bbq sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Line a large, rimmed baking sheet with parchement paper.
  3. Cut the potatoes into 8 wedges. They should be less than 1/2 inch thick on the wide edge; if any are thicker, cut them in half again. Wipe the wedges dry with a paper towel to absorb excess starch.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the potato wedges with the oil and toss to coat.
  5. Transfer wedges to the baking sheet, ensuring that they don't overlap. Try to have them not touch if possible.
  6. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes. Remove and flip with a fork or tongs, then bake for another 15 minutes. While the potatoes are baking, mix the mayo and bbq sauce in a bowl. The potatoes should appear brown and crispy on the edges and be fork-tender.
